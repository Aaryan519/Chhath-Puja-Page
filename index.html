Chhath Puja Celebration

About Chhath Puja

Chhath Puja is a significant festival dedicated to Surya, the Sun God, and Chhathi Maiya. Is is celebrated with great devotion in the states of Bihar, Jharkhand, and parts of Uttar Pradesh and Nepal.

Rituals:

  • Worship prepare prasad, including fruits, thekua, and other traditional dishes.
  • They fast and offer arghya to the setting and rising sun.
  • Rituals are performed by standing in water bodies like rivers and ponds.

Chhath Puja emphasizes devotion, purity, and graditude towards nature.
